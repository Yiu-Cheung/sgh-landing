# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: April 12, 2026**

Smart Gallery Hub ("SGH," "we," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What Smart Gallery Hub collects

### The desktop application

Smart Gallery Hub is a **local-first desktop application**. Your images, videos, metadata, and AI-generated content stay on your computer. We do not upload, transmit, or store any of your files on our servers.

- **No telemetry.** SGH does not phone home.
- **No cloud storage.** All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your machine.
- **AI processing is local.** When using Ollama, inference runs entirely on your hardware. If you choose to connect an external AI provider (e.g., OpenAI-compatible API), your data is sent to that provider under their privacy policy — not ours.

### The website (smartgalleryhub.com)

This website is a static site hosted on Cloudflare Pages. We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregate traffic data without cookies or personal identifiers.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.

### Purchases

Payments are processed by **Polar** (Polar Software Inc.), which acts as our Merchant of Record. When you purchase Smart Gallery Hub Pro, Polar collects your name, email, billing address, and payment information. We do not store your credit card details.

Polar's privacy policy: [https://polar.sh/legal/privacy](https://polar.sh/legal/privacy)

### Support emails

If you email us at support@smartgalleryhub.com, we receive your email address and message content. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

## How we use your data

- **Purchase data** (name, email): to deliver your license key and provide support.
- **Support emails**: to respond to your questions.
- **Anonymous web analytics** (if enabled): to understand which pages visitors view.

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

## Data retention

- **License data**: retained as long as your license is active.
- **Support emails**: retained for up to 2 years, then deleted.
- **Web analytics**: anonymous, aggregate data only; no personal data retained.

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (email support@smartgalleryhub.com)
- **Object** to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **support@smartgalleryhub.com**.

## Cookies

This website does **not** use cookies.

## Children's privacy

Smart Gallery Hub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
